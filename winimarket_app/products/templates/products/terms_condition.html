5. Escrow System

WiniMarket operates an escrow-based payment system to protect both buyers and sellers.

  • Buyer funds are held securely in escrow after successful payment.
  • Sellers are notified only after payment is confirmed.
  • Funds remain in escrow until delivery is confirmed by the buyer.
  • Upon confirmation of delivery, escrow funds are released to the seller.

If delivery is not confirmed, escrow funds remain protected while disputes are reviewed.
