WiniMarket Support

We’re here to help you resolve issues quickly and safely.

At WiniMarket, we are committed to providing reliable support for buyers and sellers. If you experience any issues related to orders, payments, deliveries, or your account, please follow the guidance below.

How We Can Assist You

  • Order and payment issues
  • Delayed or missing deliveries
  • Account access or security concerns
  • Seller or product disputes
  • Refunds and escrow-related questions

How to Get Support

1

Check Your Orders

Visit your Orders page and review the order status. Many issues can be resolved directly from the order details page.

2

Contact Support

If your issue persists, send us an email with your order ID and a brief description of the problem.

Resolution & Follow-up

Our support team will review your request and respond within 24–48 hours.

Important Notes

  • Payments are protected through our escrow system.
  • Do not share your password or verification codes with anyone.
